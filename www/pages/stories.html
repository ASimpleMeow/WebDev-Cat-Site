'The Empire Cats Building' Finally Constructed!

Finally we are seeing 'the empire cats building' constructed for the cats in America! It's five stories(boxes) high and is made out of the finest cardboard, can accommodate up to ten cats! Fully fitted with all their hourly necessities and sleeping luxury's. It's construction has been officially completed this December of the year 2015 and is said to last for five decades, it's capable of withstanding earthquakes up to the magnitude of 9.2 on the Richter scale and is completely water-proof due to the addition of micro-material to repel water particles! Truely the future is here!

Reunited After 8 Long Years!

This miracle happened on the 7th of December this year, after taking care of this cat near his store for two months, the man decided to bring him to the Marin Humane Society to investigate if he had an owner. Thanks to the micro-chip they managed to contact the owners, Nancy and Stephen Payne.
Amazingly they managed to reunite with their long lost cat who got lost as a kitten.
